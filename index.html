About the Project

Experience the timeless classic of Jose Rizal's Noli Me Tangere like never before. This interactive educational game brings the novel to life through immersive gameplay, engaging storytelling, and historical accuracy. Step into the world of 19th century Philippines and explore the themes of freedom, justice, and national identity through interactive gameplay and character-driven narratives.

Project Objectives

1
Design and develop an interactive and immersive educational game titled "Noli Me Tangere: Interactive Educational Game and Storytelling Experience", which enhances student engagement with the novel's themes and historical context.
2
Implement game-based simulations and interactive storytelling to allow students to explore Noli Me Tangere through interactive dialogues, character interactions, and in-game events that accurately depict key moments in the novel.
3
Adapt the novel's complex themes, historical context, and characters into an engaging game format while ensuring historical and literary accuracy in the game's narrative, environment, and character interactions. To guarantee content fidelity, the game's summaries, dialogue scripts, and quizzes are rigorously reviewed against the Pinagyamang Pluma 9 textbook. This material is further checked and approved by the Filipino subject matter expert, Sir Alex Arada, ensuring the game's content is not only entertaining but is also an accurate and reliable academic resource aligned with the Grade 9 curriculum.
